## Abstract
This work presents a machine learning-based approach for analyzing and improving the accuracy of B2B courier charge estimation. Various regression models were evaluated, and the Random Forest Regressor achieved the best performance, helping identify discrepancies between predicted and actual courier charges while improving billing transparency and cost optimization in logistics systems.
